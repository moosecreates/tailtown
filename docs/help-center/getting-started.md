## üîê Logging In

### First Time Login

1. Navigate to your Tailtown URL (e.g., `https://yourresort.canicloud.com/login`)
2. Enter your email address and password provided by your administrator
3. Click **Sign In**

### Forgot Your Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the instructions to create a new password

## üìä Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see the **Dashboard** - your command center for daily operations.

### Key Metrics

The dashboard displays important metrics at a glance:

From the dashboard, you can:
- View upcoming check-ins and check-outs

## üë• Managing Customers

### Adding a New Customer

1. Click **Customers** in the sidebar
2. Click the **+ ADD NEW CUSTOMER** button
3. Fill in the required information:
   - First Name and Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Address (optional but recommended)
4. Click **Save**

### Viewing Customer Details

1. Go to **Customers**
2. Use the search bar to find a customer by name, email, or phone
3. Click on the customer's name to view their full profile

### Customer Profile Includes:

- Contact information
- Emergency contacts
- All pets associated with the customer
- Reservation history
- Payment history
- Special notes

## üêæ Managing Pets

### Adding a Pet

1. Navigate to the customer's profile
2. Click **Add Pet** in the Pets section
3. Enter pet information:
   - **Name** - Pet's name
   - **Type** - Dog, Cat, or Other
   - **Breed** - Select from dropdown (searchable)
   - **Age/Birthdate**
   - **Weight**
   - **Gender**
4. Add important details:
   - **Vaccination Status** - Upload vaccine records
   - **Medical Notes** - Allergies, medications, conditions
   - **Behavior Notes** - Temperament, special needs
   - **Feeding Instructions**
5. Click **Save**

### Pet Profile Features

Each pet profile includes:
- **Photo** - Upload a profile picture
- **Medical History** - Vaccinations, vet visits
- **Behavioral Notes** - Play preferences, temperament
- **Dietary Information** - Food type, allergies, feeding schedule
- **Reservation History** - Past and upcoming stays

### Quick Pet Search

Use the **Pets** page to:
- Search all pets by name or breed
- Filter by species (Dog, Cat, etc.)
- View vaccination status at a glance
- See which pets have upcoming reservations

## üìÖ Creating Reservations

### New Reservation

1. Click **Boarding & Daycare** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ NEW RESERVATION**
3. Select or create a customer
4. Choose the pet(s) for the reservation
5. Select dates:
   - **Check-in Date & Time**
   - **Check-out Date & Time**
6. Choose service type:
   - Boarding (overnight stays)
   - Day Camp (daytime only)
   - Training
   - Grooming
7. Select a kennel/suite (or let the system auto-assign)
8. Add any extras:
   - Extra playtime
   - Grooming services
   - Special treats
   - Medication administration
9. Review pricing
10. Click **Create Reservation**

### Managing Existing Reservations

From the **Boarding & Daycare** page:
- View all reservations in calendar or list view
- Filter by status (Pending, Confirmed, Checked In, Checked Out)
- Click any reservation to view details
- Modify dates, services, or kennel assignments
- Process check-ins and check-outs

### Check-In Process

1. Find the reservation (search by customer or pet name)
2. Click **Check In**
3. Verify pet information and special instructions
4. Confirm vaccination records are up to date
5. Note any items brought by the customer (toys, food, etc.)
6. Complete check-in

### Check-Out Process

1. Find the reservation
2. Click **Check Out**
3. Review services provided
4. Add any additional charges if needed
5. Process payment
6. Print receipt
7. Complete check-out

## üè† Managing Kennels

### Viewing Kennel Availability

1. Go to **Kennels** in the sidebar
2. View all kennels/suites with their:
   - Current status (Available, Occupied, Maintenance)
   - Size and capacity
   - Amenities
   - Current occupant (if any)

### Kennel Types

Tailtown supports different kennel types:
- **Standard Kennels** - Basic accommodations
- **VIP Suites** - Premium accommodations with extra space
- **Cat Cabanas** - Specialized for feline guests
- **Group Play Areas** - For social dogs

### Assigning Kennels

Kennels can be assigned:
- **Automatically** - System finds best available kennel
- **Manually** - Choose specific kennel during reservation
- **Reassigned** - Move pets between kennels if needed

## üí° Quick Tips

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Ctrl/Cmd + K** - Quick search (customers, pets, reservations)
- **Ctrl/Cmd + N** - New reservation
- **Ctrl/Cmd + /** - Open help

### Best Practices

1. **Keep Vaccination Records Updated**
   - Upload vaccine certificates for each pet
   - Set reminders for expiring vaccines
   - Require up-to-date vaccines for boarding

2. **Use Notes Effectively**
   - Add behavioral notes for staff safety
   - Document special feeding instructions
   - Note customer preferences

3. **Regular Check-Ins**
   - Review today's schedule each morning
   - Confirm all check-ins and check-outs
   - Update reservation statuses promptly

4. **Communication**
   - Use the announcement feature for staff updates
   - Keep customer contact information current
   - Document all customer interactions
