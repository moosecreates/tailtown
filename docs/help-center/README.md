## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

**Q: Can I access Tailtown on my mobile device?**  
A: Yes! Tailtown is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets.

**Q: How do I add multiple pets for one customer?**  
A: Go to the customer's profile and click "Add Pet" for each pet.

### Reservations

**Q: Can I book multiple pets in one reservation?**  
A: Yes! Select multiple pets when creating the reservation.

**Q: What if a kennel isn't available for my dates?**  
A: The system will show all available kennels. You can also use the waitlist feature.

**Q: How do I handle early check-ins or late check-outs?**  
A: Modify the reservation times and any additional fees will be calculated automatically.

### Billing

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**  
A: Credit cards, debit cards, and cash. Some locations also accept checks.

**Q: Can I split payments between multiple payment methods?**  
A: Yes, use the split payment feature during checkout.

**Q: How do I issue a refund?**  
A: Go to the invoice and click "Issue Refund" - partial or full refunds are supported.

## ğŸ”§ Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Issue: Can't log in**
- Verify your email and password are correct
- Check if Caps Lock is on
- Try resetting your password
- Clear your browser cache

**Issue: Page won't load**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser

**Issue: Can't find a customer or pet**
- Use the search bar at the top
- Check spelling of names
- Try searching by phone number or email
- Verify you're looking in the correct date range

**Issue: Reservation won't save**
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check for date conflicts
- Verify kennel availability
- Check vaccination requirements are met
